\begin{ccRefClass}{Compute_anchor_3<RegularTriangulation_3>}

\ccDefinition

The affine hulls of a Regular simplex and its dual Voronoi cell
intersect in a single point, called the center. The closest point in a
Regular simplex (Voronoi cell) is called the anchor point of the
simplex (cell). The anchor point coincides with the center of the
simplex (cell) or with the center of one of its faces. 

The class \ccRefName\ for a given Regular simplex (Power cell) with
the center of which simplex it coincides.
