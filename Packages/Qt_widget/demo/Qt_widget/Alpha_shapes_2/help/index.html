Generate Delaunay triangulation

To generate a Delaunay triangulation, go to Edit menu and choose "Generate Triangulation" button, or press Ctrl+G combination keys.

 

 

Insert a new vertex

Left Click on the "Input point" layer. Left Click anywere on the application's drawing area (the black rectangle) to insert the vertex. To insert more than one vertex, keep clicking on the rectangle without deactivating the "Input point" layer.
