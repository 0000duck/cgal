\begin{ccRefConcept}{MeshTraits_2}

\ccDefinition

In addition of the requirements of the concept \ccc{ConformTraits_2},
the concept \ccRefName{} has to provide two special predicates
\ccc{Compute_squared_minimum_sine}, and \ccc{Is_bad}.

\ccRefines

\ccc{ConformTraits_2}

\ccTypes

\ccNestedType{Compute_squared_minimum_sine_2}{Constructor object. Must 
  provide the operator \ccc{double operator()(Point_2 a, Point_2 b,
    Point_2 c)} that computes the squared minimum sine of the triangle 
  formed by the three points a, b, c.}
\ccNestedType{Is_bad}{Predicate object. Must provide the operator
  \ccc{bool operator()(Point_2 a, Point_2 b, Point_2 c)} with tells if 
  the triangle formed by by the three points a, b, c satisfies the
  wanted criteria for mesh triangles.}
