\ccDefinition

In addition of the requirements of the concept \textbf{QUEL\_MODEL\_ ?},
the concept \ccRefName\ has to provide a numeric field type \ccc{FT},
a type \ccc{Vector_2} and several constructors on \ccc{Vector_2} and
\ccc{Point_2}, a predicate on angles \ccc{Angle_2}, and two special
predicates \ccc{Compute_squared_minimum_sine}, and \ccc{Is_bad}.
