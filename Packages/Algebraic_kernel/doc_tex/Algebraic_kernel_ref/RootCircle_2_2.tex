\begin{ccRefConcept}{AlgebraicKernel_2_2::RootForCircles_2_2}

\ccDefinition

The \ccc{RootForCircles_2_2} concept represents algebraic numbers that can
store the roots of systems of two \ccc{PolynomialForCircles_2_2}
equations of degree 2 in two variables \ccc{x} and \ccc{y}.
