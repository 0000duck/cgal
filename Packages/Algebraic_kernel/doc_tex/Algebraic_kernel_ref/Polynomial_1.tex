\ccDefinition

The \ccc{Polynomial_1_2} represents bivariate polynomials of degree 1 whose coefficients are of type \ccc{RT}, a model of \ccc{RingNumberType}. 

\ccTypes

\ccc{RT} is supposed to be a model of \ccc{RingNumberType}, given 
by \ccc{Rational_traits<FT>}.\footnote{again...}
