\begin{ccRefConcept}{AlgebraicKernel_d_1} 

\ccDefinition

The \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1} concept is meant to provide the
algebraic functionalities on univariate polynomials of general degree
$d$ necessary as a basis for the curved kernel.

A model of \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1} is supposed to provide:

\ccTypes

\ccNestedType{Coefficient}{
A model of \ccc{IntegralDomain} and \ccc{RealEmbeddable} 
}

\ccNestedType{Polynomial_1}{
A model of \ccc{Polynomial_1}, 
where \ccc{CGAL::Polynomial_traits_d<Polynomial_1>::Innermost_coefficient}
is \ccc{Coefficient} 
} 

\ccNestedType{Algebraic_real_1}{
A model of \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::AlgebraicReal_1}
}

\ccHeading{Functors}

\ccNestedType{Solve_1}{A model of the concept
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::Solve_1}.} 

\ccNestedType{Sign_at_1}{A model of the concept
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::SignAt_1}.}

\ccNestedType{Is_square_free_1}{A model of the concept 
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::IsSquareFree_1}.}
\ccGlue

\ccNestedType{Make_square_free_1}{A model of the concept

\ccGlue

\ccNestedType{Is_coprime_1}{A model of the concept 
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::IsCoprime_1}.}
\ccGlue

\ccNestedType{Make_coprime_1}{A model of the concept 
\ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d_1::MakeCoprime_1}.}
