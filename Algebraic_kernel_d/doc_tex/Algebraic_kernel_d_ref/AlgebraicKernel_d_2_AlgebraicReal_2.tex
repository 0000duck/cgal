\begin{ccRefConcept}{AlgebraicKernel_d_2::AlgebraicReal_2}

\ccDefinition

A Model of this concept represents the roots of a system of two polynomial 
equations in two variables \ccc{x} and \ccc{y} that are models of 
concept \ccc{AlgebraicKernel_d::Polynomial_2}.

The model is required to be \ccc{LessThanComparable} with repsect to a 
lexicographic order.
