\ccFunction{bool
  is_theta_monotone(const Circular_arc_3<SphericalKernel> & a,const Sphere_3<SphericalKernel>& sphere);}
{Tests whether the arc $a$ is $\theta$-monotone, i.e. the intersection of
any meridian anchored at the poles \ccc{sphere} and the arc $a$
is reduced to at most one point in general, and two points if a pole of \ccc{sphere} is an endpoint of the arc.
Note that a bipolar circle has no such arcs.
\ccPrecond{\ccc{a} lies on \ccc{sphere}, and the supporting circle of \ccc{a} is not bipolar.}
}
